Biography

English: Altito (Ishay Altit) is an Israeli trance producer who began his musical journey in 2018. After years of diving deep into music production and sound design, he decided in 2023 to share his music with the world. His style blends Goa Trance with Retro Dark, featuring hypnotic melodies and deep psychedelic vibes. Each track is a personal journey, representing his unique mark in the trance scene. He has released an EP on the international label Timewarp Records and collaborated with several Israeli trance artists along the way.
